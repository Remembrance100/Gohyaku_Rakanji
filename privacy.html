Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Please review the following before purchasing access to the Memorial Tour Guide.

Refund Policy

As this is digital content available immediately upon purchase, all sales are final. We do not offer refunds for any reason once access has been purchased.

Access Period

Access is valid for 24 hours from your first use, with unlimited use during that window.

Information We Collect

We do not require an account. To provide the tour, we store your language and text-size preferences, and a temporary access token, in your browser's local storage — never on our servers.

Payment Information

Payments are processed directly by Stripe or PayPay. We never see or store your card or payment account details — only confirmation that a payment was completed.

Tour Content

Tour text, images, and audio are loaded from our content server as you browse. Viewing the tour does not require sharing any personal information with that server.

Permitted Use

The Service is intended for personal use by visitors to Gohyaku Rakanji during their visit.

Data Retention

Your access token expires automatically 24 hours after your first use and is not stored anywhere except your own device.
